Granite

Also known as Natural stone. It is mined from quarries around the world, as sediment solidified over centuries, and is now available to grace our kitchens and other indoor and outdoor areas.

Quartz

Quartz surfaces are made of up to 93% quartz and are designed with longevity in mind.Resistant to stains, scratches and cracks, quartz is impervious to heat and cold and to common household chemicals and makes for a beautiful, highly durable surface.

Marble

Marble is a semi-precious stone. Formed in much the same way as Granite it has more exotic qualities. It is also the most expensive counter top option available.
